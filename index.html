How Code Brought Us Together

Our story began in a virtual classroom dedicated to the ones and zeros of software engineering. Over a year of learning, debugging, and collaborating, we discovered we had more in common than just a love for clean code (ps: only one of us cared about clean code. can you guess who?). We found a shared logic in our hearts and the perfect syntax for a lifelong partnership. As all projects with collaborators, there will sometimes be conflicts and there will be resolutions, and we're happy to be figuring life out together.

Ovo is a pharmacist and software developer from Iyede, Isoko-North, Delta State.

Chee-Zaram is a public health and software practitioner from Alike, Obowo, Imo State.
